## Project Overview

This document provides a clear breakdown of the website development project for Horizon Adventures, a premier adventure tourism company based in Pretoria, South Africa. The website serves as your complete online presence, showcasing your services, tours, destinations, and making it easy for customers to contact you.

---

## Website Breakdown & Features

### 1. **Website Pages Created**

Your website includes the following pages:

#### Main Pages:
- **Home Page** - The main landing page with an eye-catching hero section, overview of your services, trip types showcase, and customer testimonials
- **Tours Page** - A dedicated page showcasing all your featured tours, activities, and couples retreat options
- **Destinations Page** - Information about all the amazing destinations you offer tours to
- **Gallery Page** - An interactive photo and video gallery where visitors can browse through your adventure experiences
- **About Page** - Your company story, mission, vision, statistics, and an embedded promotional video
- **Contact Page** - A contact form where customers can easily reach out to you, plus your contact information
- **Services Page** - Detailed information about all the services you offer
- **Testimonials Page** - A dedicated page showcasing customer reviews and feedback
- **Trip Types Page** - Information about different types of adventure trips you offer
- **Featured Destinations Page** - Highlights of your most popular destinations
- **Privacy Policy Page** - Legal documentation for your website

#### Individual Tour Pages:
- Cape Town Tour Page
- Durban Tour Page
- Hartbeespoort Tour Page
- Mpumalanga Tour Page

### 2. **Key Features & Functionality**

#### Navigation & Layout:
- **Navigation Menu** - A professional navigation bar that stays visible as visitors scroll, with a mobile-friendly menu that slides in smoothly
- **Footer** - A comprehensive footer with links to all important pages and your company information
- **Page Headers** - Beautiful header sections on each page with your branding

#### Interactive Features:
- **Photo & Video Gallery** - An advanced gallery where visitors can:
  - Browse through photos and videos of your adventures
  - Click to view images and videos in full screen
  - Swipe through content on mobile devices
  - Navigate using keyboard or touch gestures
- **Animated Trip Showcase** - Dynamic presentations of different trip types with smooth animations
- **Tour Cards** - Attractive cards displaying your tours with hover effects
- **Testimonials Display** - A rotating carousel showing customer reviews
- **Services Grid** - Interactive cards showcasing your service offerings
- **Contact Form** - A user-friendly contact form that validates information and prevents errors
- **Cookie Consent Banner** - A banner that complies with privacy regulations

#### User Experience Features:
- **Mobile-Friendly Design** - The website looks great and works perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers
- **Smooth Animations** - Professional animations that make the website feel modern and polished
- **Fast Loading** - Images and content are optimized to load quickly
- **Easy to Use** - The website is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate for all visitors
- **Accessible** - The website can be used by people with disabilities, including keyboard navigation

### 3. **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**

Your website has been optimized so that:
- It appears in Google and other search engines
- Each page has proper titles and descriptions
- A sitemap has been created to help search engines find all your pages
- Images are optimized for fast loading and search engine visibility

### 4. **Design & Branding**

- Custom color scheme matching your brand
- Professional typography that's easy to read
- Consistent design throughout all pages
- Modern, clean, and professional appearance
- Adventure-themed visual elements
- Integration of your professional photography
